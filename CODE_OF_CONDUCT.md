# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

This project welcomes contributions, guest posts, and bug reports from anyone. We
pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and
expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its readers.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments, public or private.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the
project maintainer at **jeremy@intentsolutions.io**. All complaints will be
reviewed and investigated and will result in a response appropriate to the
circumstances. The maintainer is obligated to respect the privacy and security
of anyone who reports an incident.
