Anthropic's Claude Tag ships a genuinely strong agent into Slack with zero infrastructure, but the substrate is Anthropic's: the sandbox, the memory, and the audit log all run on their infra. CCSC and AGP make the opposite trade: fine-grained deterministic policy, per-tool-call human-in-command approval, and a signed, offline-verifiable audit you check with your own key. Both are legitimate. Pick by who must own the substrate.
