## What helps a first email

- **What you're trying to do**, in plain language. Not "explore an AI strategy" — "we have 6,000 PDFs of inspection reports and want to ask them questions in Slack."
- **Where you are now.** Codebase, vendor stack, who's on the team, what's already failed.
- **What "done" looks like.** Even a rough sketch — production for real users, internal demo, board update — is enough to scope the conversation.
- **Timing.** Soft deadline, hard deadline, or open-ended.

I don't need a polished brief. A four-paragraph email beats a 30-page deck for getting to a useful first call.
